Coaching Staff

Emma Randall
Head Coach

Emma Randall coaches at Columbia and the NYC Regional Training Center. She holds the USA Wrestling Gold level certification — held by fewer than 100 coaches in the United States — and competed at the national level before moving into coaching.

She earned her B.A. and M.S. in Sports Psychology from Lock Haven University. Prior to Columbia, she served as Program Director at Beat the Streets NYC. She is a founding board member of Wrestle Like A Girl, an advisory board member of the Sports Bra Project, and serves on the USA Wrestling Division I Women's Wrestling committee.

erandall@nycrtc.com
Maya Letona
Assistant Coach · CC '24

Maya Letona was a member of the U.S. Senior National Team and competed at the U.S. Olympic Trials for Team USA. After graduating from Columbia College in 2024, she returned to the program as assistant coach.
